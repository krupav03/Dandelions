Little Rosy

Let's PADdle for her to be comfortable.

Since September 2021, we have been working on menstrual hygiene campaign distributing free sanitary pads for around 750 women till date and raised menstrual awareness in them. Covering the areas around Kaapula Uppada, Paradesipalem, Vellanki and Boddapalem villages within the limits of Visakhapatnam.

Annapurna

Our heartful thanks to everyone who supported us from September 2021 to till date. We are happy that we could give back to the community. For the months from February to April, Annapurna extended her helping hand to the people in need. We distributed around 605 food packets along with 760 water bottles, and 30 blankets. The food is cooked in a very hygienic atmosphere. We distributed the blankets only in our first event and understood that it exhausts the funds and on top of that, most of them are having atleast one with them. For that reason, we chose to distribute only food and water bottles.

Little Joy

Our heartful thanks to everyone who supported us from September 2021 to till date. We are happy that we could give back to the community. For the months from May to August, Little Joy extended her helping hand to the children in need. We have provided three orphanages stationary and served cooked food for two days, In addition to that some of them required groceries and we have extended our hand to help them with the necessary requirements.
